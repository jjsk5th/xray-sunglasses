Resume

Jack was the 2nd hire at GrubHub and witnessed it go from tiny start-up to post-IPO firsthand. After nine years there he left to explore other ideas and has since done consulting and design work for numerous start-ups.

2015

User Experience Design • Mobile Design • Prototyping • Interaction Design • Visual Design • Data Visualization • Product Brainstorming

2014

2013

Jack started at GrubHub a year out of college, witnessing GrubHub's growth from a small startup to post-IPO. As the 1st designer, he maintained a central design role throughout nine years. Involved in everything from marketing and branding, to the user experience, optimization. and front-end development.

Lead Designer

Co-led UX team. Built and led initial optimization team, resulting in a +20% change in conversion. Oversaw visual design of all GH products across mobile and desktop platforms.

Creative Director

Being the leader of the creative team, he had a significant role in building and defining the brand, as well as helping build the initial creative team.

Designer & more

Being a bright-eyed youth new to the working-world, he had to make a path by creating one and helped design and build the early iterations of the product. Found himself working with people much smarter than he is.

Key Accomplishments

  • Built and led the initial Growth team, resulting in significant improvements of key metrics.
  • Large role in building and defining the GrubHub brand.
  • Oversaw visual design across all GrubHub products and platforms.
  • Oversaw the (re)design and (re)building of mobile web app.
  • Alongside the Director of UX (who's from california), he built and lead the user experience team consisting of 20+ designers and researchers.
  • Helped lead and oversee the integration of the GrubHub and Seamless design teams when the two companies merged in 2013.
  • Responsible for the product design of both brands post-merger.
  • Announced the new brand with a redesigned website as well as a national ad campaign. This was a very busy time.
  • Designed, and later on oversaw the creation of numerous national transit-based ad campaigns.
  • Acted as brand steward to ensure the brand was accurately represented across all consumer touch points.
